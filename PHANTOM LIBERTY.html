CYBERPUNK 2077
PHATOM LIBERTY

FREEDOM ALWAYS COMES AT A PRICE

Phantom Liberty is a new adventure that adds a touch of thriller and espionage to Cyberpunk 2077. Once again play as V, a cyberpunk mercenary, and embark on a perilous mission of espionage and political intrigue to save the NUSA president. In the dangerous neighborhood of Dogtown, you'll have to forge alliances in the midst of a whirlwind of shattered dreams and political power plays. Will you be up to the task?

MISSION BRIEFING

OBJECTIVE TARGET LOCATION ABILITIES AND GEAR

Your mission is to rescue the former CEO of Militech, now the President of NUS, Myers. He crashed in the dangerous Dogtown area while aboard Space Force One. You need to find him before he is located by hostile forces and ensure his safety. This mission is crucial for the stability of NUS, so we need you to act quickly.

The target location is the Dogtown area, a place filled with danger. This area is a forgotten industrial zone, now a nest of gangs and criminals. You need to find Myers in this dangerous environment and get him out.

As a cyber-enhanced mercenary, you will need to utilize your abilities and gear to complete this mission. You will need to infiltrate high-security buildings, eliminate enemies, and confront experienced professionals head-on. Please ensure that your gear and skills are ready to face any challenges that may arise.

DOSSIERS

MYERS

The former CEO of militech and the current President of the New United States of America. Currently in her third term, Myers is in the uneviable position of running a country at time when power is wielded by coprorations. While traveling on the Space Force One, Myers crash lands in Dogtown, triggering a power struggle with far-reaching consequences.

SONGBIRD

A talented netrunner and President Myers right hand. There aren't many people who can keep up with her abilities. Songbird's talent makes her a valuable asset to NUSA, one the country needs on its side to defend the nation's precarious position. Leaving her post in not an option.

REED

Solomon Reed is an experienced FIA agent who's proven himself countless times in covert intellingence missions. He knows better than anyone how to tap into the conttless webs of spies and netrunner, how to extract information, and how to break into even the most tightly guardec places. His loyalty and sense of duty is difficult to shke.

ALEX

Once an aspiring braindance actress, ALEX was recruited to the NUSA's Federal Intellingence Agency by Solomon Reed. A talented shapeshifter, she also happens to have a mean streak that her time in Dogtown did nothing to temper. But as long as she's playing a part, her true personality won't peek through.
